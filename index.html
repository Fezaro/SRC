Who we are

Solidarity Child Rescue Centre is a small Christian centre located on our own half-acre plot n Kasarani, Nairobi, the capital city of Kenya. We are officially registred as a charitable organisation and a charitable children institution. We are a small organization with a big heart, concerned with the plight of the street children, orphaned and vulnerable children in Kenya.
